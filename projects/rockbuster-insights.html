Project Overview

Core Question

How can we use historical rental data to identify our most valuable customers, optimal content categories, and priority markets for a successful streaming launch against Netflix and Amazon Prime?

Business Value

A comprehensive business intelligence framework with customer segmentation, content strategy recommendations, and market prioritisation for RockBuster's digital transformation.

Data Analysis & Visualisations

Geographic Revenue Analysis

Revenue distribution by country, revealing the optimal demographic profile for digital service targeting.

Genre Performance Deep Dive

Detailed revenue analysis across all movie categories, confirming the multi-genre strategy for subscriber retention.

Strategic Business Insights

  • US market is the primary launch target — largest customer base with established brand recognition
  • Sports and Animation categories drive the highest revenue — priority for digital content licensing
  • Customers aged 37–39 generate the highest revenue per country — primary demographic for campaigns
  • Top 20% of customers generate 60% of revenue — exclusive launch incentives are essential
