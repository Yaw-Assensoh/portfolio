RockBuster Stealth Data Analysis

Business Intelligence & Strategic Recommendations

Project Overview

Business Objective: To guide Rockbuster Stealth's digital transformation from physical rental stores to a global streaming service by leveraging existing customer data to maximize subscriber acquisition and content strategy effectiveness.

Core Question: "How can we use historical rental data to identify our most valuable customers, optimal content categories, and priority markets for a successful launch against established competitors like Netflix and Amazon Prime?"

Key Deliverable: A comprehensive business intelligence framework with customer segmentation, content strategy recommendations, and market prioritization for the digital launch.

My Role & Process:

  • Data Collection & Cleaning: Used SQL to extract and Python/Pandas to clean customer, film, and sales data
  • Exploratory Analysis: Analyzed sales trends and customer demographics to find initial patterns
  • Pattern Identification: Segmented high-value customers and identified top-performing regions and film genres
  • Insights Generation: Recommended targeted loyalty programs and data-backed market expansion strategy

Strategic Business Insights

  • Market Prioritization: The United States represents the largest customer base, making it the primary target for the digital launch, with India and China as secondary expansion markets.
  • Content Strategy: Sports and Animation categories drive the highest revenue, suggesting these should be focal points for digital content acquisition and original programming.
  • Demographic Targeting: Customers aged 37-39 generate the highest revenue per country, indicating this age group should be the primary target for marketing campaigns.
  • Genre Diversification: While Sports leads in revenue, strong performance across Sci-Fi, Action, and Comedy genres suggests a balanced content library will maximize subscriber retention.

Streaming Strategy & Market Positioning

Critical Digital Launch Findings:

  • Revenue Concentration: Top 20% of customers generated 60% of revenue—these "Power Users" should receive exclusive launch incentives to ensure platform adoption.
  • Content Competitive Edge: Sports ($4,836 revenue) and Animation categories outperformed all others, representing Rockbuster's unique strength vs. Netflix's drama/comedy focus.
  • Geographic Launch Sequence: US market has 4.2x more customers than second-place India, making phased launch (US → India/China → EU) the lowest-risk approach.

Digital Transformation Recommendations:

  • For Launch Strategy: Implement "Power User" migration program offering 6 months free to top 20% customers (who generate 60% of revenue) to secure critical mass and word-of-mouth marketing.
  • For Content Acquisition: Allocate 40% of licensing budget to Sports content and 25% to Animation—domains where Rockbuster already outperforms streaming giants—to establish immediate competitive differentiation.
  • For Market Expansion: Execute phased geographic rollout: Month 1-3: US (existing base), Month 4-6: India/China (strong recognition), Month 7-12: European markets with localized 37-39 age targeting.
  • For Customer Retention: Develop "Genre Journey" recommendation algorithm that moves Sports/Animation viewers to related Sci-Fi/Action content (high-performing secondary genres) to increase average viewing time by 35%.

Strategic Impact:

  • Enabled targeted loyalty programs for high-value customers in the US market
  • Pinpointed Sports and Animation as priority content categories for digital acquisition
  • Identified 37-39 age demographic as primary target for digital marketing campaigns
  • Provided data-backed content strategy balancing high-performing genres with audience diversity
  • Guided international expansion strategy with clear market prioritization based on existing customer base
