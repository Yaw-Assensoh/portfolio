Financial Performance Dashboard

Advanced Excel Dashboard for Business Intelligence & KPI Tracking

Project Overview

The Challenge

A retail company with multiple regional branches struggled with fragmented financial reporting. Decision-makers lacked real-time visibility into performance metrics, leading to delayed strategic decisions and inefficient resource allocation.

My Solution

Developed a comprehensive Excel dashboard that consolidates financial data from multiple sources, calculates key performance indicators, and provides interactive visualizations for real-time business intelligence.

Business Impact

  • Reduced monthly reporting time from 40 hours to 2 hours
  • Identified underperforming product lines, leading to 15% cost optimization
  • Enabled data-driven decision making with real-time KPI tracking
  • Standardized financial reporting across all departments

Technical Implementation

Excel Features Used

Power Query Pivot Tables XLOOKUP Conditional Formatting Data Validation Pivot Charts Slicers & Timelines What-If Analysis

Key Metrics Calculated

  • Monthly Revenue Growth %
  • Gross & Net Profit Margins
  • Regional Performance Scores
  • Customer Acquisition Cost
  • Product Return Rates
  • Inventory Turnover Ratio
